Fish Bordelaise and Potato

Fish Bordelaise with (or without) potatoes. Potatoes can be mashed, rosti or any other form.

Ingredients

  • Fish Bordelaise
  • Potatoes of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat the oven.
  2. Put in the Fish. If your potatoes need to be prepared in the oven as well, put them in alongside each other.
  3. Wait until done. Pull out.
  4. Possibly let them cool down.
  5. Have fun and a full stomach.
